English

Overview

Portfolio Dashboard ("the App") is a personal investment portfolio tracker. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Authentication

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. When you sign in:

  • Google verifies your identity and returns a secure token to the App.
  • We read only your basic profile (name, email, profile picture) to display in the UI.
  • We do not store your Google credentials or password.
  • We request access to Google Drive only for the appDataFolder scope (a hidden folder exclusive to this App).

Data Storage

Your portfolio data (stock positions, watchlists, trade records) is stored in your own Google Drive — specifically in the hidden appDataFolder, which is invisible to you in the regular Drive interface and inaccessible to other apps.

  • We do NOT store any personal data on our servers.
  • The Vercel serverless functions process requests in memory only; nothing is persisted server-side.
  • Deleting your data is as simple as revoking the App's access in your Google Account settings.

Stock Quotes

Real-time and delayed stock quotes are fetched from Yahoo Finance on demand. Your portfolio positions are never sent to Yahoo Finance — only ticker symbols are used in the query.

Cookies & Tracking

  • We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.
  • Google Sign-In may set cookies managed by Google. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics services.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only external services involved are Google (authentication & Drive) and Yahoo Finance (quotes).

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data lives in your Google Drive, you have full control:

  • To delete your portfolio data: go to Google Account Permissions, find Portfolio Dashboard, and remove access. This deletes the appDataFolder and all its contents.
  • To stop using the App: simply stop signing in. No action needed on our end.

中文說明

隱私政策摘要

以下為中文說明，供參考。如有歧義，以英文版本為準。

驗證方式

本應用使用 Google 帳號登入，我們只讀取您的基本個人資料（姓名、Email、頭像）用於介面顯示。我們不保存您的 Google 帳號密碼或憑證。

資料存放位置

您的投資組合資料（股票持倉、自選清單、交易記錄）存放在您自己的 Google Drive，使用隱藏的 appDataFolder（其他應用程式及您本人均無法在 Drive 介面中看到此資料夾）。

  • 我們的伺服器不儲存任何個人資料。
  • Vercel 伺服器函式僅在記憶體中處理請求，不進行任何持久化儲存。

股票報價

即時或延遲的股票報價來自 Yahoo Finance。我們只向 Yahoo Finance 發送股票代碼進行查詢，您的持倉數量與成本等個人資料不會傳送至 Yahoo Finance。

Cookie 與追蹤

  • 我們不使用廣告 Cookie 或追蹤像素。
  • Google 登入可能設置由 Google 管理的 Cookie，詳情請參閱 Google 的隱私政策。
  • 我們不使用任何第三方分析服務。

資料共享

我們不出售、出租或分享您的個人資料給任何第三方。涉及的外部服務僅有 Google（登入與 Drive）以及 Yahoo Finance（報價）。

刪除資料

由於所有資料都存放在您的 Google Drive，您擁有完全控制權：前往 Google 帳戶權限頁面，找到 Portfolio Dashboard 並移除存取權限，即可刪除 appDataFolder 及所有資料。
